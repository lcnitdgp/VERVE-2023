About

Literary Circle

Literary Circle is the club in NIT Durgapur, which gives the college an extra dimension of creative expression in the midst of technical unilateralism and gives the students of the college an opportunity to transcend the ordinary and mundane.

About Verve

The static buzz in the air has been charged into a symphony of restless joy and it is time to find a new rhythm. We're past the days of staring into iridescent screens, it is time to witness grandeur in the flesh, to revive the creativity buried away.

The Literary Circle is proud to unveil the official logo for the eighteenth edition of the annual Youth cum Cultural Festival of NIT Durgapur, VERVE.

The best weekend of the year awaits you, spread across four enthralling days, from 16th to 19th March, 2023. From a thrilling Treasure Hunt to an enchanting Prom, and a plethora of events in between, Verve XVIII is here.

Carve the chronicles of your escapade.
